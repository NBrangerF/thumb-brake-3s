# Hook Criteria Library V0

## Admission Criteria

- The opening must be readable in the first second.
- Non-product openings must include a product_bridge_rule that reconnects to product value within 3-5 seconds.
- A pattern must be transferable across products or at least one clear category.
- A pattern must be generatable through first_frame, video_reference, or hybrid execution.
- Pure decoys remain observations and do not enter recommended Pattern Cards.

## Research Basis

The resource package uses official research surfaces as observation structures, including TikTok Top Ads, TikTok Keyword Insights, TikTok Creative Center, Meta Ad Library, and Google Ads Transparency Center. It stores reusable grammar only, not copied videos, raw transcripts, usernames, or exact creative lines.

## Quality Metrics

- Clarity: criteria are concrete enough to guide a prompt or storyboard.
- Transferability: the pattern can move across products or one durable category.
- Product Bridge Strength: the hook naturally leads to product value.
- Generation Readiness: reference requirements and generation mode are explicit.
- Diversity Contribution: the pattern adds a new mechanism or category angle.
