# Video Case Gallery

These short clips are included as public README examples for the kinds of first-seconds, visual tension, and product-bridge moments Thumb Brake 3s is designed to plan.

They are reference examples for creative structure, not generated output guarantees.

## How To Read These Clips

When reviewing or adding examples, focus on the creative mechanism:

- What stops the thumb in the first second?
- What visible evidence makes the viewer understand the scene?
- Does the product enter as a tool, proof object, ritual object, or scene resolution?
- Is the opening specific enough to avoid generic ad language?
- Could the mechanism be reused safely without copying a creator's exact line or private asset?
